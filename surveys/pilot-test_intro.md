# Pilot Test Survey

*This markdown file shows the structure of the survey that people completed during the pilot test.*

# Preliminary Questions

**FREQ:** In the past few months, how often, if at all, did you use a generative AI platform (like ChatGPT or Claude) to chat about your personal experiences, emotions, or well-being? *Examples include conversations where you discussed feelings, sought advice about relationships, reflected on your mental and physical health, or talked about your day, even if the conversation started on a different topic.*

-   Daily or multiple times a day

-   Several times a week

-   About once a week to a few times a month

-   About once a month

-   Never

**PLAT-PSY:** Which generative AI platform have you been using most frequently for psychological support or advice? Please [select only one]{.underline} (*If they answered 'Never' to the previous question the wording would be:\
"*Besides psychological support, which generative AI platform have you been using most frequently or are you most familiar with?")

-   Claude

-   Google Gemini

-   Microsoft Copilot

-   Meta AI

-   DeepSeek

-   Character.AI

-   Replika

-   Other

**TR/DT:** Thinking about [selected platform], how much do you trust/distrust it for psychological or emotional support?

-   Very little

-   A little

-   Moderately

-   Quite a bit

-   Very much

-   Completely
